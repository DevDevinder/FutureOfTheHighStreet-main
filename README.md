# Future Of The High Street

Welcome! This notebook helps visualise and analyse city data - including postcode locations, affluence heatmaps, transport routes, CCTV analytics, and business access.

No coding knowledge is required.  
If you'd like the full step-by-step guide with videos and pictures for further understanding, visit: 
[documentation site](https://future-of-the-high-street.github.io/nu-collab-notebook.github.io/)



---

## Section 1 - Setup

Initialises the notebook environment and loads city-specific data.  
> Always run this first.

---

## Section 2 - Postcode Display

Displays postcodes on the map based on different boundaries such as:
- City wide.
- City centre.
- Shopping districts.

---

## Section 3 - Affluence Heatmaps

Visualises postcode-level heatmaps based on:
- affluence.
- Household density.

---

## Section 4 - Transport

Provides travel route visualisation and analysis:
- Compare travel times (walk, drive, cycle, bus).
- Show nearest postcodes to areas of interest.
- Utilises **BusNet4** integration for  bus route details

---

## Section 5 - CCTV Insights

Explore footfall and traffic data:
- Select datasets and camera locations.
- Generate charts by hour/day/date.
- Map camera locations with total or average traffic counts.
- View bus routes that pass by camera locations.

---

## Section 6 - Business & Bus Stop Proximity

Map businesses and surrounding bus stops:
- Filter by business type.
- display buisness info.
